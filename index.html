About Me

Hi there! My name's David. I'm a fourth year student at the University of California, Irvine expecting to graduate in June 2018. Thanks to UCI's Capstone Experience program, I've had the awesome privilege to work with a company called SendGrid. My team and I worked under the mentorship of the company's engineers, and by the end of 10 weeks, we were able to successfully design, implement, and test a large scale system that would reduce the average latency for mail processing.

As a software developer, I love using my skills in front-end and back-end technology to help make every-day people's lives better. I love working with companies that have the highest potential outreach to customers, and above all, I love doing things that have meaning and that are practical. I am currently interested in full-time software engineering positions and internships located in Irvine, San Francisco, Los Angeles, and Seattle.

When I'm not constantly staring at a computer screen, you'll find me playing the piano or going out for walks. Or staring at my phone while I walk. Because I still play Pokemon Go.
