About Me

Hi there! My name's David. I'm a fourth year student at the University of California, Irvine expecting to graduate in June 2018. Thanks to UCI's Capstone Experience program, I've had the awesome privilege to work with SendGrid. My team and I are currently working under the mentorship of SendGrid's engineers, and we're in the process of designing, implementing, testing and deploying a large scale system that will reduce the average latency for mail processing significantly.

As a software developer, I love using my skills in front-end and back-end technology to help make every-day people's lives better. I love working with companies that have the highest potential outreach to customers, and above all, I love doing things that have meaning and that are practical.

When I'm not constantly staring at a computer screen, you'll find me playing the piano or going out for walks. Or staring at my phone while I walk. Because I still play Pokemon Go.
